Actions are core of Tegon. They help you write and automate workflows. 

<Note>
We use [Trigger](https://trigger.dev) to manage and deploy the actions you write. 
</Note>

Before digging deeper into the details of writing actions, you should read the [fundamentals of actions](/actions/core-concepts) to understand what actions are and how they work.
